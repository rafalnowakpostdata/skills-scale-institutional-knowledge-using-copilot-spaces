# OctoAcme — Project Kickoff Checklist & Template

## Purpose
Ensure all projects have clear role assignments, aligned stakeholders, and an effective launch with shared understanding of goals, scope, and responsibilities.

## Pre-Kickoff Preparation (PM + PdM)

- [ ] Draft Project One-pager (problem, goal, success metrics, timeline)
- [ ] Identify core and extended roles needed for this project
- [ ] Confirm availability and assign owners for each role
- [ ] Create stakeholder list with communication preferences
- [ ] Schedule kickoff meeting (60–90 min, invite all core roles + key stakeholders)
- [ ] Prepare agenda and supporting materials

## Kickoff Meeting Agenda (60–90 minutes)

### 1. Welcome & Context (5 min)
- Briefly explain why this project matters and business context
- Introduce participants and their roles

### 2. Problem Statement & Goals (10 min)
- **PdM leads**: present problem, objective, and success metrics
- Clarify what success looks like and how we'll measure it

### 3. Scope & Timeline (10 min)
- **PM leads**: present high-level timeline, milestones, and phasing
- Identify key dependencies and constraints

### 4. Roles & Responsibilities (15 min)
- **PM leads**: confirm role assignments and clarify responsibilities
- Review **[OctoAcme Personas](octoacme-roles-and-personas.md)** for extended roles (Design Lead, QA Lead, Release Engineer, Security Reviewer, etc.)
- Discuss interaction patterns and decision-making authority
- Clarify escalation paths and who to contact for specific decisions

### 5. Initial Risks & Dependencies (10 min)
- **PM leads**: present preliminary risk list and mitigation plans
- Discuss cross-team dependencies and coordination needs
- Identify stakeholders who must be kept informed

### 6. Communication Plan (10 min)
- Confirm meeting cadence (standups, syncs, reviews, demos)
- Establish decision-log location and update process
- Confirm status report frequency and distribution

### 7. Next Steps & Q&A (10 min)
- Confirm planning start date and first planning session
- Answer questions and confirm alignment
- Close with recap and next action items

## Post-Kickoff Deliverables

**Due within 2 business days:**
- [ ] Updated Project One-pager with confirmed dates and team
- [ ] Risk Register with initial risks and owners
- [ ] Decision Log finalized from kickoff
- [ ] Communication Plan confirmed
- [ ] First planning session scheduled

**Due before planning starts:**
- [ ] Project board / backlog structure created
- [ ] Success metrics dashboard initialized (if applicable)
- [ ] Design kick-off completed (if Design Lead engaged)

## Kickoff Success Criteria

- [ ] All participants understand the problem and desired outcome
- [ ] Roles and responsibilities are clear; no ambiguity on who decides what
- [ ] Success metrics and timeline are agreed
- [ ] Key risks and dependencies are identified
- [ ] Next steps and planning schedule are confirmed
- [ ] Team is energized and aligned
